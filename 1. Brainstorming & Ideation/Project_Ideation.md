# Phase 1: Brainstorming & Ideation — UCab Platform

## 1. Executive Problem Statement
In fast-paced urban environments, travelers and daily commuters often face unpredictability when booking cab rides: hidden pricing surges, lack of transparent vehicle categories, delayed driver assignments, and inflexible payment mechanisms. Furthermore, traditional cab platforms lack human-centric touches such as booking in-ride refreshments or contributing directly to driver welfare funds.

**UCab** bridges this gap by delivering a streamlined, highly transparent MERN stack cab booking platform that emphasizes instant fare calculations, real-time dispatch tracking, and customizable travel perks.

---

## 2. Empathy Map & User Personas

### Persona A: Sarah (The Punctual Professional)
* **Needs:** Needs a cab in under 5 minutes to reach the airport without price ambiguity.
* **Pain Points:** App crashes during payment, unclear vehicle arrival times.
* **Solution in UCab:** One-click location selection, real-time ETA calculation, and automated saved payment checkout.

### Persona B: Rajesh (The Fleet Dispatch Manager)
* **Needs:** A centralized executive dashboard to monitor active rides and assign operational status to fleet vehicles.
* **Pain Points:** Fragmented spreadsheet tracking and lack of visibility on completed revenue.
* **Solution in UCab:** Real-time KPI dashboard, instant availability toggles, and multi-filter trip management.
